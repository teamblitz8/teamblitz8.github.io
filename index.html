Who are we?

We are Team Blitz of Saint Olave's Grammar School, a group of 4 dedicated team members with a burning passion of STEM and DT. We all came together over the months, collaborating with each other with our own various different skillsets and thinking. We thoroughly enjoy the hands-on experience the F1 in Schools club provides us and the teamwork & collaboration skills we have developed on our journey, helping us get to where we are with our car, portfolio and brand today!

What is F1 in Schools?

F1 in Schools is a STEM-based fun activity with the goal of introducing students to STEM subjects in an engaging way, using the theme of F1 to encourage students to compete. Students create a miniature F1 car with F1-style features to maximise aerodynamics, thinking through aesthetics, manufacturing, sketches and designing on CAD, designing a portfolio to document their journey and even developing a brand to display at the competition and raise funds for getting the best resources possible!
